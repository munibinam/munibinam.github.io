## Research Focus {#research}
My work bridges **applied econometrics**, **climate science**, and **environmental policy** to deliver actionable insight on three fronts:

1. **Measuring Climate Extremes** – How methodological choices (e.g., spatial interpolation) alter estimates of heat stress and precipitation intensity.
2. **Impacts on Agriculture** – Quantifying yield and land‑use responses and evaluating on‑farm adaptation strategies.
3. **Water Quality & Demand** – Linking weather shocks to drinking‑water violations and residential consumption patterns.
