## Research Overview {#research}
I apply applied econometrics, spatial analysis, and high‑resolution climate data to answer policy‑relevant questions at the intersection of **climate science, agriculture, and water resources**. My current work focuses on three themes:

1. **Measurement Error in Climate Extremes**   – How methodological choices (e.g., spatial interpolation) bias estimates of heat stress and precipitation intensity.
2. **Agricultural Impacts & Adaptation**       – Quantifying yield and land‑use responses to extreme weather and evaluating adaptation strategies.
3. **Water Quality & Demand under Stress**     – Linking climate shocks to drinking water violations and residential consumption patterns.
